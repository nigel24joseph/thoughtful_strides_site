Support for Couples

Restoring trust, rebuilding connections

Couples often seek support when communication breaks down or the relationship feels strained. This might include:

  • Ongoing conflict or frequent arguments
  • Difficulty communicating or feeling unheard
  • Loss of connection, closeness, or intimacy
  • Trust issues or recovering from betrayal
  • Navigating major life transitions together
  • Differences in values, expectations, or goals
  • Feeling stuck in repeating patterns
  • Wanting to strengthen the relationship and reconnect

Working with couples provides a space where both people can feel heard and understood, often for the first time in a while. My role is to support you both to step out of unhelpful patterns and have more open, constructive conversations.

In sessions, we focus on understanding what’s happening beneath the surface of conflict or disconnection. This includes how each of you experiences the relationship, how communication unfolds, and how patterns may be repeating over time.

I work in a balanced and respectful way, ensuring both perspectives are given equal attention. Together, we identify what’s getting in the way and develop new ways of communicating, responding, and reconnecting.

The focus is not on blame, but on understanding and change. This may involve rebuilding trust, improving communication, and strengthening connection so the relationship feels more stable, supportive, and aligned with what you both want.
