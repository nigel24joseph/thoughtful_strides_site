Getting Started

How to book an appointment

1

Get in Touch

Reach out via the contact form, phone, or email. A GP referral is not required, but is recommended if you'd like to access Medicare rebates.

2

Discovery Call

A brief 10-minute call to discuss your concerns and goals, and confirm we're the right fit for your needs.

3

First Appointment

Your initial session focuses on understanding your background, current concerns, and what you'd like to achieve from therapy.

4

Ongoing Support

We develop a personalised treatment plan and work together at a pace that suits you — in person or via Telehealth.
